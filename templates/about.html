Our Mission

RecoverAI was developed to revolutionize how athletes approach injury recovery. By leveraging advanced machine learning, we provide accurate predictions of recovery timelines and personalized rehabilitation recommendations.

Our goal is to help athletes recover faster and smarter by identifying key factors that influence recovery duration and providing actionable insights to optimize the healing process.

The Technology Behind RecoverAI

Ensemble Learning

Our model combines multiple algorithms including Gradient Boosting, Random Forest, and SVM to achieve maximum accuracy and reliability in predictions.

Feature Engineering

Advanced feature engineering creates 15+ derived features including training load, recovery ratios, and risk indices for comprehensive analysis.

SMOTE Balancing

Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique ensures the model learns equally well from all types of recovery outcomes.

Feature Selection

SelectKBest algorithm identifies the 20 most predictive features from our engineered feature set for optimal model performance.

Recovery Outlook Guide

Excellent (< 25% risk)

Timeline: 3-5 days

Optimal recovery profile. Continue current practices and maintain good habits.

Good (25-50% risk)

Timeline: 5-10 days

Good recovery potential with minor optimizations. Focus on sleep and warm-up routines.

Moderate (50-75% risk)

Timeline: 10-21 days

Proactive measures recommended. Consider reducing training frequency and intensity.

Extended (> 75% risk)

Timeline: 21-42 days

Immediate action required. Consult sports medicine professional and prioritize rest.
